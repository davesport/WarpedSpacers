About the Spacers

The Warped Spacers write, read, and watch works of "speculative fiction," which includes sci-fi, fantasy, horror, and the paranormal. We aim to improve our writing through group critique. In our group, we regularly submit pieces of writing for review, and discuss possible edits. We also discuss writing advice, ask each other questions and tips, and share exciting writing news with each other! Most importantly, we support each other's writing goals and work to make them happen.

How does it work?

Members of the Spacers meet each first and third Monday of the month via Zoom. Together, they critique submissions and discuss ways to improve their writing. Yearly the Spacers create an anthology of short stories: rough drafts are due by June 1st, and final drafts by September 30th. The anthology is published in early December, and distributed by Christmas :)
