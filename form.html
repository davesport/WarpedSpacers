Thanks for your interest!

If you have any questions or comments for us, please write them in the comment section of the form.

For this form: There are many kinds of writers. There aren't right or wrong answers on this form, instead your responses help us get to know you better!

The Warped Spacers meet twice a month on Zoom, which means you can join our meetings from anywhere. We aren't always taking new authors in order to manage the size of our group, but please contact us so we can be in touch! We also can consult one submission from someone who is not a current member but would like feedback on their work.
